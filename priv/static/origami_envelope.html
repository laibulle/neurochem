  1. Print on A4, scale 100% (no fit-to-page). Use plain paper.
  2. Step 1: Lay the printed sheet face up. Fold the bottom edge up so the bottom fold line (marker 1) aligns with the mid panel — fold along the dashed line at ~99mm. Crease sharply.
  3. Step 2: Fold the top edge down to cover the bottom fold (marker 2), aligning it to the second dashed line at ~198mm. Crease sharply.
  4. Step 3: Turn the sheet over. Fold the two side corners slightly in (about 15–20mm) to create triangular flaps so the envelope closes neatly.
  5. Step 4: Tuck the top flap into the pocket formed by the bottom fold, or secure with a small sticker / glue dot.
  6. Note: The address block (right-hand box) will be on the outside of the envelope after folding; place recipient address there before printing for direct mailing.
